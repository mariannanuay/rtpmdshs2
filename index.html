Welcome to RTPM-DSHS!

Founded in 1988 in Dumaguete City to provide advanced science education. The school moved to its permanent campus in 1991, after land was donated by the family of Ramon Teves Pastor. Today, RTPM-DSHS is part of the Regional Science High School system.

About Our School

History

RTPM-DSHS was established in 1988 to provide advanced science and mathematics education. Today, it serves students across Negros Oriental and Central Visayas, offering a strong STEM foundation.

Vision

To be a center of excellence in science and mathematics, shaping learners who are creative, responsible, and ready to make a difference.

Mission

To spark curiosity with challenging STEM programs, grow integrity and personal skills, and build strong connections with families and communities.

Our Location

Academic Programs

Junior High School

Follows the K–12 curriculum with enrichment in science, mathematics, research, and technology. Students must maintain high grades to remain in the program.

Senior High School

Offers the STEM strand with advanced courses for students pursuing STEM-related college programs or careers.

School Activities

Competitions and Achievements

Math and Science Contests

Students join academic competitions such as math and science quizzes, research presentations, and STEM challenges.

Speech and Arts Competitions

Students participate in speech choirs, poster-making, and creative arts events. RTPM‑DSHS placed 2nd Runner‑Up in the 2024 Speech Choir Competition at a Dumaguete City literary festival.

Sandurot Festival Pasundayag

An annual cultural celebration featuring street dance and showdown events.

  • 2023: 1st Place in the School Category Showdown, including Best in Choreography and Best Instrumentalist
  • 2024: Overall Champion among school contingents, top placement in the Pasundayag showdown

Other Recognitions

RTPM‑DSHS has been honored in DepEd Region VII Pasidungog Awards, including Most Outstanding Secondary School (Large Category) in 2023.

Academic Achievements

  • Research and STEM Recognition: Recognized in DOST‑SEI Youth Innovation and Research Competitions for science, technology, and engineering projects.
  • Regional and National Competitions: Top placements in Math and Science Olympiads locally and regionally.
  • DepEd Regional Awards: 2023: Most Outstanding Secondary School (Large Category) in DepEd Region VII Pasidungog Awards.
  • Graduating Student Achievements: Graduates achieve high university entrance scores and STEM scholarships.
  • Consistent STEM Performance: Students maintain high standards: 87% in major subjects, 85% in minor subjects.
